# Explainer Video — Script & Storyboard

> A production-ready script for a ~90-second product explainer. Pair the **voiceover (VO)** with a screen recording of the [live demo](https://kintsugi-health-os.vercel.app) and/or the [pitch deck](pitch-deck.html). Brand: calm, credible, warm — "gold seams on dark." Music: minimal, hopeful, unhurried.

- **Runtime:** 75–95s
- **Aspect:** 16:9 (1920×1080); also export a 9:16 social cut
- **Tone of voice:** reassuring, intelligent, never clinical or alarmist
- **Tagline lockup (open & close):** *Kintsugi Health OS — Investigation, not diagnosis.*

> **Kintsugi turns your scattered health data into understanding you own — investigation, not diagnosis.**

## Voiceover (clean read, ~140 words)

> Your health leaves a trail — sleep, symptoms, labs, the days you felt off. But it's scattered across portals and apps, and no one sees the whole picture. So we google, and we worry.
>
> Kintsugi changes the question — from *what's wrong with me?* to *what's actually going on?*
>
> A ninety-second check-in builds your record. A deterministic Health Detective finds the patterns and correlations in your data — and frames them as questions to investigate, never verdicts.
>
> Test a theory with a simple experiment. Watch what changes. And when you see a doctor, walk in with a clear, evidence-based case — so ten minutes finally count.
>
> It's private by design, and it's yours — export everything, or delete it all, anytime.
>
> Kintsugi Health OS. Investigation, not diagnosis. Try the live demo today.

## Shot list (for screen recording the demo)

Record at 1920×1080, light theme, demo account (`demo@kintsugi.health`):

1. `/checkin` — complete a check-in (shows the capture flow).
2. `/dashboard` — Health Momentum + indices grid.
3. `/investigate` — a finding framed as a question.
4. `/graph` — the knowledge graph (let edges settle).
5. `/cases` (or build one) — the specialist case summary.
6. `/reports` — scroll a weekly/monthly report.
7. `/profile` — the export + delete controls (privacy beat).

Tip: slow the cursor, pause ~1.5s on each key element, and use gentle 200–300ms crossfades. Overlay the captions above in a light sans (e.g., Inter), gold accent `#D7A94B` on a dark lower-third.

---

## Compliance guardrails for the edit

- Never imply diagnosis, treatment, or a medical claim. Keep "investigation, not diagnosis" prominent.
- Use **synthetic demo data** only; no real patient data.
- Include a closing card / description disclaimer: *"Not a medical device. Does not provide medical advice. In an emergency, contact local emergency services."*
